The 4 Phases of Project Management:

Planning

Determine the viability of the investment and obtain an agreement to pay, and plan the control of the project.

Execution

Plan the specifics of the project, and perform all activities to complete a successful project.

Implementation

Go live with the project work product.

Closure

Complete the documentation

Stakeholders:

Stakeholder

A stakeholder is any entity with an interest in the outcome of a project.

Customer

The customer is a stakeholder that is paying for the service or product.

User/end-user

The user/end-user is the entity that consumes or utilizes the product or service.

Service Provider (Internal Service Provider/Shared Services Unit/External Service Provider)

This entity provides services to customers and users to enhance business productivity.

RACI Matrix/Model

A tool used to identify stakeholders and their level of involvement in a project or activity.

RACI Matrix/Model

R - Responsible for correct execution
A - Accountable for final result
C - Consulted to provide additional knowledge & info
I - Informed or kept up to date regarding process
